Peace Ground. Holidays. No Mercy.

They play only on holidays.
Their home is Peace Ground though peace was never guaranteed.

When this team first stepped onto this ground, they lost more than matches.
They lost tosses, confidence, momentum and sometimes the courage to check the scorecard.

Captains changed.
Strategies evolved.
The jersey stayed the same.
Results? Mostly unchanged.

Peace Ground became known not for victories,
but for heavy defeats served fresh every holiday.

But something changed.

Today, this team doesn't collapse it competes.
It doesn't surrender it fights till the last over.
And sometimes… it wins.

No shortcuts.
No change of ground.
No waiting for luck.

They simply learned how to survive Peace Ground.

From losing everything
to finally earning respect.

That's not luck.
That's progress.
